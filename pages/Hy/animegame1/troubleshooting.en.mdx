# Troubleshooting

## Error 4214
### "Unable to connect to server. Please check your network settings."
![4214 (image)](../../../public/commonerror.jpeg)\
To fix this, you must download the [patched mhypbase](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/970380775264518244/1049282295145713754/mhypbase-latest.zip).
1. Rename the original `mhypbase.dll` and `mhypbase.ini` file to `mhypbase.dll.bak/mhypbase.ini.bak` and put in the patched one.
2. Extract the [patched mhypbase.dll and mhypbase.ini file](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/970380775264518244/1049282295145713754/mhypbase-latest.zip)
3. Copy and paste the patched mhypbase.dll and mhypbase.ini to the root folder of GI. (*anime game*, the folder with GenshinImpact.exe)
