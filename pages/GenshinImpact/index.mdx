## Setting up the launcher
Once you are done downloading, open the YuukiPS launcher and click on the "Config" tab.

On the Game Folder option, click on the "Choose" button.

When you click it, it will show a file explorer window. Locate your Genshin Impact folder (the folder with GenshinImpact.exe, not GenshinImpact_Data). Select the game's folder.

Now, go back to the "Connect" tab.

Click on the "Mode Online" checkbox

Turn off the "Auto Reload" option.

Set the port to anything else (ex. 4242), go back to the "Config" tab, and click "Save"

Select on "YuukiPS", and click the large "Connect" button.
