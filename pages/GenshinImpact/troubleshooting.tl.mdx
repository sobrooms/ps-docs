# Troubleshooting

## Error 4214
### "Unable to connect to server. Please check your network settings."
![4214 (image)](../../public/commonerror.jpeg)\
Para maayos ito, kailangan mong i-download ang [patched na mhypbase](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/970380775264518244/1049282295145713754/mhypbase-latest.zip).
1. Palitan ang pangalan ng orihinal na `mhypbase.dll` at `mhypbase.ini` file sa `mhypbase.dll.bak/mhypbase.ini.bak` at ilagay ang patched.
2. I-extract ang [patched mhypbase.dll at mhypbase.ini na file](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/970380775264518244/1049282295145713754/mhypbase-latest.zip)
3. Kopyahin at i-paste ang na-patch na `mhypbase.dll` at `mhypbase.ini` sa root folder ng GI. (*anime game*, ang folder na may GenshinImpact.exe)
